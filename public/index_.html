About Deporte Conex

Deporte Conex is an online recruiting platform which focuses on providing opportunities for international high school student athletes, and success on the court or field for American university sports coaches.

  • Athletes
  • Recruiters

We offer a recruiting platform which connects student-athletes in Latin America and the Caribbean with American university coaching staff. These connections can lead to scholarships and greater opportunities in the future for student-athletes on the recruiting platform


We cannot guarantee any student-athlete will receive athletic scholarships. We can guarantee however, that we will provide as many chances possible for each and every student-athlete to get a fair chance to get exposure and to get noticed by university coaches and recruiters.


Sign up now. Your path to being recruited starts here. It is free and takes less than 120 seconds. Reserve your profile today and show your commitment to university coaches and recruiters!

You get to recruit from a new and large talent pool, helping build impressive recruiting classes. Deporte Conex helps you win the recruiting battle by providing a new supply of talent!
